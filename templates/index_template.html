About

Use this app to predict the price of properties listed for sale in Melbourne. This will allow you, as a potential property buyer or investor, to assess whether or not a current house listing is over or undervalued. Helping you to decide on the offer you make.

Who wouldn't want to live in a safe but affordable neighbourhood? That's why the Price Predictor also takes crime rates from the suburb of your choice into account. We want to make sure you can afford your new home without having to give in on safety and security.

What's the catch? There is none! As opposed to other online price predictors, you will not be required to pay any fees or disclose personal details. You can do your own research and make a well informed decision about your potential new home.

To use this service, simply click 'PREDICT NOW' from anywhere on the app. Copy and paste the URL from your favourite Domain listing and we'll do the rest. Please ensure the listing is Melbourne based and states the number of cars, bedrooms and bathrooms.
